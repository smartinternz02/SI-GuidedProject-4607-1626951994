CRUDE OIL PREDICTION

As an analytical tool for this purpose, the study has utilized an integrated econometric model developed in the past for estimating supply and demand of crude oils and petroleum products as well as petroleum trade flows for various countries of the world with a main focus on Asia, with necessary modifications and improvements provided to make the model more accurate and elaborate. Demand for oil is inelastic, therefore the rise in price is good news for producers because they will see an increase in their revenue. Oil importers, however, will experience increased costs of purchasing oil. Because oil is the largest traded commodity, the effects are quite significant. A rising oil price can even shift economic/political power from oil importers to oil exporters. The crude oil price movements are subject to diverse influencing factors.
